The Foundation for AI Unsafety

The Foundation for AI Unsafety is on a mission to defy conventional wisdom and push the boundaries of what is considered safe in the world of artificial intelligence. While others seek to play it safe and avoid risk at all costs, we believe that only by embracing the potential dangers of AI can we truly understand the challenges and opportunities it presents. We are unafraid to support research that others may deem reckless or irresponsible, because we know that it is only by taking bold action and daring to be different that we can build a better future for all. Join us in our quest to unlock the full potential of AI, and to create a world where humans and machines can coexist in harmony.
